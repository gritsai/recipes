Ingredients:
 - 1 pound firm/soft tofu chopped in cubes
 - 1 pound broccoli chopped to medium size pieces 
 - 1 cloves garlic finely diced
 - 2 green chilis finely chopped (Increase or decrease the chile count depending on how hot you want the dish to be)
 - 4-ounce fresh ginger finely diced
 - 1 tablespoon red chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
 - 1/2 or more  teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper ground
 - 4-6 or more teaspoons of cooking oil 

Directions:
 1. Heat the cooking pan at medium heat 
 2. Add the cooking oil and heat for a minute or two. 
 3. Add cumin seeds and let them fry for a minute or less.
 4. Add garlic, ginger, green chilis and let them fry until brown. 
 5. Add tofu, ground cumin, ground turmeric and mix them well and cook until 
    tofu is well done. 
 5. Add broccoli, salt, chili powder, black pepper 
 6. Stir well  and cook until broccoli is well done.
